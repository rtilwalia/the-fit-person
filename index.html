About Us

‘Nirogi kaya’ literally meaning “disease free body” is a perfect balance of a healthy body, mind and soul. Fitness is not only about being disease free, it’s about the spark and glow within the mind to keep yourself active.

The fit person is guiding start-up blog for fitness freaks who are looking for something healthy and motivating tips for every day.

Find all what you need to stay healthy and fit on our page with latest and exciting fitness routines, healthy snacks, and what not.

Discover how working out can be fun and exciting with proper guidance and scientific facts.
